# Daily AI Frontier Update — 2026-03-31

- **OpenAI outlined how the OpenAI Foundation will deploy capital (Mar 24):** OpenAI says the Foundation plans to invest at least $1B over the next year across life sciences, jobs/economic impact, AI resilience, and community programs. **Why it matters:** This signals frontier labs expanding from model releases into large-scale societal deployment and resilience infrastructure.
- **Google DeepMind published empirical work on harmful AI manipulation (Mar 26):** DeepMind released a study toolkit based on 9 studies and 10,000+ participants to measure whether AI can deceptively shift beliefs/behavior in high-stakes domains. **Why it matters:** Manipulation risk is moving from abstract concern to measurable safety benchmark.
- **Anthropic released its March 2026 Economic Index report:** Using privacy-preserving usage analysis, Anthropic reports broader Claude adoption, more task diversification, and evidence that high-tenure users achieve higher-value outcomes. **Why it matters:** Real-world productivity gains appear to depend increasingly on user skill and workflow maturity, not just raw model quality.
- **Anthropic published findings from 80,508 user interviews across 159 countries:** The large multilingual qualitative study maps what people want from AI, major concerns, and where expectations diverge from current usage reality. **Why it matters:** Product/safety strategy is increasingly being shaped by large-scale user preference data, not only benchmark performance.
